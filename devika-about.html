✦ how I got here

The accidental researcher.

I didn't plan to be a UX researcher. Honestly, I just sort of ended up here and then realised it was exactly where I was supposed to be.

I was doing design work and randomly landed a job in design research. But the moment I started interviewing people, synthesising insights, and watching those insights actually shape what got built I was hooked. Turns out I'd been doing research-brained thinking my whole life. I just didn't have a name for it.

Now I work at the intersection of people, culture, and product asking the questions that others assume are already answered.
